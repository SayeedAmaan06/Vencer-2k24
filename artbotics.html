Event Details

An AI art contest focuses on text-to-image generation which can be an exciting way to explore the capabilities of AI in transforming written descriptions into visual art. Participants will use AI tools to generate artwork based on provided text descriptions. The goal is to create visually compelling images that accurately represent the given descriptions. The contest will evaluate creativity, adherence to the prompt, and the quality of the generated images.

Rounds:- The event consist of total 3 rounds ,the details will be provided on the spot.

Rules

  • The participant must register for the event.
  • Maximum number of participants in a group is 2
  • Event begins at 11.00 am, participants must report 15 mins prior.
  • The participants must carry their own Laptops.
  • Event consist of total 3 Rounds each of 30 mins
  • Themes and topics will be provided on the spot
  • All work must be original and created during event
  • Copying prompts from generative AI (ChatGPT, Bing etc.) is prohibited.
  • Instructions and more details regarding all the rounds will be provided by the coordinators.
  • Images should be created wholly using TEXT to IMAGE AI tools.

Submission Guidelines

  • High quality images must be submitted via E-mail (email address will be specified later) .
  • It must be submitted before deadline.

Judging Criteria

  • Evaluation based on aesthetic appearance.
  • How well the images align with the given theme or topic.
  • Depth and creativity in the interpretation of the theme.
  • Clarity, detail, and realism of the generated images.
