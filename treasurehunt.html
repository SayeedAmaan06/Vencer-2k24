Treasure Hunt

Register Now

Event Details

Gather your team and get ready for an epic treasure hunt! As you race against time, decode clues, and work together, the thrill of the chase intensifies. It's all about quick thinking and collaboration as you strive to claim the ultimate prize. Join the excitement and let the adventure begin!

Rounds

ROUND 1 :

  • This consist of Finding the treasure through MAP

ROUND 2:

  • This consist of Technical based Searching for clues in the Riddle.

ROUND 3:

  • This Consist of Riddles based where Participants Should Search for the clues.

ROUND 4:

  • : This Consist of Technical Round Where participants have to Find the Clues based on their Technical Skills.

Rules

  • All team must have 2-3 members.
  • There will be a total of 4 rounds.
  • Game will be conducted 2 days .
  • Each day consists of 2 rounds.
  • The detailed rules of the round will be given by the coordinators before the round starts.
  • The organizing team reserves the right to make changes to certain rules.
  • Each round will be governed by time limits, compelling the team to strategize and complete assigned challenges within the specified time frame to progress."
  • The team's performance in each round may earn them points, and the accumulation of these points will ultimately decide their standing.
  • Open to all departments.
  • Decisions taken by the judges will be treated as fair and final.
