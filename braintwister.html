Brain Twister

Register Now

Event Details

“Exercise your mind with our brain teaser game! Challenge yourself with a variety of puzzles, riddles, and logic games designed to test your problem-solving skills. From Sudoku to word games and pattern recognition challenges, our game offers endless hours of fun while sharpening your mental acuity. Ready to give your brain a workout?’’

Eligibility

  • Open to anyone willing to participate.
  • Each group must contain 2 participates.

Registration

  • Registration process is in both online mode and on spot registration.
  • Each group must provide necessary details like Name, Contact information while registering.
  • Registration fees is 150/- per group.

Competition Format

  • Competition is consist of 4 round first round has 30mins of time.
  • Each round top scorers are eligible for next round.
  • Evaluation is based on completeness.
  • Judge’s decisions are final and binding.
  • Other rules are informed on spot.

Rules

  • Participants must adhere to the principles of fair play and sportsmanship.
  • Any form of cheating, harassment or misconduct will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Participates must aware about the updates of event, and organiser are not responsible for any technical issues or unforeseen circumstances that affect the competition.
  • If anyone have a any type of doubt you can clarify with coordinators.
  • Participants are required to carry their college ID Cards.
  • Only 5-6 teams are selected for final round.
  • Audience shall not give any hints or clues to the participants.
  • Replacement of any participant of a team is not allowed after registration.
  • Replacement of any participant of a team is not allowed after registration.
  • Rules listed are subjected to change as per the discretion of the event heads and the organising committee the decision of the organising committee shall be final in all matters.
  • For further enquiry contact coordinators

Rounds

1st round

  • This round contain 25 questions and each question contain 1min and the questions are based on General knowledge, Electronics related and etc.

2nd round and 3rd round are surprise.
