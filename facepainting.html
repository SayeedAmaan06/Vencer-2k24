Face painting

Register Now

Event Details

"Explore the colorful world of face painting at our event! Join us for a day filled with creativity and fun as talented artists transform faces into vibrant works of art. Whether you're a participant or spectator, immerse yourself in this unique celebration of artistic expression and community joy."

Rules

  • **Event Duration**: The event spans 2 days.
  • **Day 1 - Free Theme**: Create any face painting design based on your chosen theme. Be creative and original.
  • **Day 2 - Surprise Theme**: Paint according to a surprise theme announced at the start of the day. Show creativity within the given theme.
  • **Materials**: Bring your own skin-safe, non-toxic face painting supplies.
  • **Timing**: Paint within specified time frames each day.
  • **Health and Safety**: Only use face paints and cosmetic-grade glitters that are specifically designed for use on skin. Avoid craft paints or other non-cosmetic products.
  • **Participation**: Pre-registration required; attendance both days mandatory for overall prizes.
  • **Conduct**: Respect others; inappropriate behavior leads to disqualification.
  • **Compliance**: Follow all event rules; non-compliance results in disqualification.
  • **Judging and Prizes**: Evaluation based on creativity, Judges' decisions are final and binding.
  • Note:- All team must have 2 members. • Each participates should bring His/Her own painting supplies ,including paints ,brush, water container and other tools and supplies necessary to complete the task
