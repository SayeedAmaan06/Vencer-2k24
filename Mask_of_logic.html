Event Details

Join us for an epic and thrilling battle in virtual reality VALORANT at our battle field in our collage fest VENCER-2k24. Gather your teams, strategize and ready to fight and witness for an intense battle between the players. Show your skills to the others and reach to the Victory in VALORANT battle.

Rounds

Round 1: Tech Trivia

  • This round will be a timed multiple-choice quiz.
  • Questions will focus on basic programming knowledge, recent advancements in AI, Machine Learning, and related emerging technologies.

Round 2: Bug Zapper

  • Identify and fix bugs containing intentional errors in provided code (C, Python, Java).
  • Earn points for each bug zapped within the time limit.

Round 3: Code Craft

  • Tackle any 3 of 5 coding problems in your preferred language.
  • Points will be awarded based on the correctness and efficiency of the solutions.

Round 4: Code Scramble

  • Participants will be presented with jumbled code snippets.
  • They need to rearrange the snippets in the correct order to achieve a desired functionality.

Round 5: Decode Pursuit (Uncharted Alley)

  • Squares of code, a hidden quest, Unlock the path, and put your mind to the test.

Rules

  • Teams can consist of 2-4 participants.
  • Each team participating in the competition requires a minimum of two laptops. These laptops must have a code editor installed that is compatible with C, Python, and Java.
  • Mobile phones can not be used during the rounds.
  • No smart gadgets are allowed until permitted by coordinator.
  • Participants are expected to maintain academic integrity and avoid plagiarism.
  • Decisions made by the coordinators and judges are final.
  • All team members are expected to interact with competition organizers and judges with courtesy and respect.
  • Disputes should be communicated calmly and professionally. Organizers will do their best to address any concerns.
